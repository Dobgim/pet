Adoption Process

Bringing a puppy home is a big step. We've made our process simple, transparent, and exciting!

Step 01

Find Your Perfect Puppy

Browse our gallery of available puppies. Filter by breed, gender, or price to find the furry friend that melts your heart.

View Gallery
Step 02

Q&A & Lifestyle Match

Once you've chosen a puppy, we will have a brief consultation. We'll ask about your family lifestyle, living conditions, and experience with dogs to ensure it's a perfect match.

Step 03

Discuss Pickup/Delivery

We'll arrange how you'll get your puppy. You can pick them up directly from our home, or we can discuss safe, trusted delivery options via a pet nanny if you are located out of state.

Step 04

Ownership Transfer & Prep

We'll handle the upfront requirements. You'll sign the ownership transfer documents, review the vet records, and receive our puppy starter guide to help you prep their new home!

Ready to Start?

Our team is here to walk you through every step. Let's make your dream of an adorable puppy a reality.
