Meet the Family

Welcome to Little Paws Boutique! We are a family-owned boutique breeder dedicated to raising healthy, happy, and well-socialized puppies.

What started as a deep love for dogs has evolved into our life's mission. We believe that every puppy deserves a loving forever home, and every loving family deserves the perfect furry companion. We specialize in small, affectionate breeds like Pomeranians, French Bulldogs, Golden Doodles, and more.

Our puppies are raised inside our home, surrounded by kids and other pets, ensuring they come to you completely pre-spoiled and ready for family life!

Our Core Values

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of care and ethics.

Health First

Every puppy is vet-checked, vaccinated, and microchipped before leaving our care. We provide a 1-year genetic health guarantee.

Socialization

Raised in our living room! Our puppies are exposed to household noises, children, and other animals from day one.

Lifetime Support

We don't just sell puppies; we build relationships. We are here to answer your questions and offer guidance for your dog's entire life.
